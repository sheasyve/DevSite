Super AI Bros - Reinforcement Learning AI for Super Mario BrosBirdFlow - Wind Simulator Extension for Blender

 Super AI Bros was an experimental processes of developing an agent to perform in the game Super Mario Bros, undertaken by a group of undergraduate students at the University of Victoria using reinforcement learning. Reinforcement learning is generally considered a necessary component for creating human-like or super-human-like artificial intelligence for a wide variety of problems. The implementation of the Super Mario Bros game was taken from the gym-super-mario-bros 7.4.0 Python package. After spending time optimising learning agent hyperparameters, it was evident that all of our learning agents, despite the changes in hyperparameters, were significantly limited by our ability to train many iterations due to limitations in computing power. This led to limits in the performance of our agents, as they were unable to learn the game to a high level of proficiency. Nonetheless, Mario was able to learn to nearly complete the first level of the game, which was a significant achievement.
